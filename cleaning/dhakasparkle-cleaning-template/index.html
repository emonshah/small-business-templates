About Us: Our Story & Commitment

Discover what drives Dhaka Sparkle and our dedication to providing exceptional cleaning services.

Dhaka Sparkle was founded with a clear vision: to transform homes and lives through professional, reliable, and eco-conscious cleaning. We believe that a clean environment is fundamental to well-being and productivity. Our journey began with a small team and a big dream to set new standards in the cleaning industry.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name, serving countless satisfied clients across Dhaka.
