Our Story

SparkleClean was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a clean, healthy, and happy home without the hassle. We saw a need for a cleaning service built on unwavering trust, exceptional reliability, and a genuine passion for sparkling results.

From day one, we committed to being different. We meticulously vet and train every professional, ensuring they share our dedication to detail and customer satisfaction. We prioritize your well-being and the planet's health by exclusively using eco-friendly, safe cleaning products. Our mission is to give you back your time, peace of mind, and a home that truly shines.

Our SparkleClean Guarantee
