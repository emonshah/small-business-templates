Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

Not at all! Our professionals arrive with their own high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning supplies and equipment. If you have a special product you'd like us to use, just let us know!

Is it safe to have your cleaners in my home?

Absolutely. Every member of our team undergoes a rigorous vetting process, including background checks and in-person interviews. They are also fully insured for your peace of mind.

How do I pay for the service?

We accept all major credit cards through our secure online payment system. Payment is processed only after your cleaning service has been completed.

Can I reschedule an appointment?

Yes, you can easily reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled service time through your online account or by contacting our support team.
